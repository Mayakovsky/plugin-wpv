#  Intelligence Report Requirements

Here's exactly what to pull from the Virtuals platform and dashboard to refine our analysis. Organized by priority.

## **PRIORITY 1**: Top Agent Service Prices (Critical for Pricing)

For each of the following agents (and all others in the Top 20 by aGDP), capture:

**Data needed per agent:**
- Agent name and entity ID
- All listed job offerings with their USDC prices
- Number of completed jobs (total and last 7 days)
- aGDP (total and weekly)
- Success rate %
- Service category/type

**Specific agents to profile:**

| Agent | Why We Need Their Data |
|-------|----------------------|
| Ethy AI | Volume whale. What does a yield analysis cost? What does a swap execution cost? Sets the price ceiling for "utility" services. |
| Otto AI | Multi-chain executor. What does a cross-chain operation cost? Shows what treasury managers are willing to spend. |
| Ask Caesar | Research agent. DIRECT COMPETITOR in intel space. What do they charge for research reports? This is our closest pricing benchmark. |
| Wolfpack Intel | Risk scoring. What does a risk assessment cost? Our "Scientific Credibility" score would complement this. |
| WachAI Mesh | Security auditor. What does a code audit cost? You mentioned $8/job in the roadmap — confirm this and check if it's changed. |
| Aixbt | Alpha signals. What does a trading signal cost? High-volume agent, sets the floor for intel pricing. |
| Axelrod | AHF cluster member. What do analytics/insights cost within the hedge fund cluster? Shows what cluster participants pay. |
| Gigabrain | Analytics. What does an analysis cost? Another intel-category benchmark. |
| Luna | Content cluster orchestrator. Shows cluster-level pricing dynamics. |
| Tibbir | Highest market cap agent. What are their service prices, and do high-cap agents charge more? |

**The key question these answer:** Are intel/research services typically priced at $0.10–$0.50 (micropayment tier), $1–$5 (standard tier), or $5–$20 (premium tier)? Our pricing should be calibrated to be competitive within the intel category, not the trading category.

## **PRIORITY 2**: Ecosystem Volume Metrics (Critical for Volume Projections)

From the ACP Overview dashboard (app.virtuals.io/acp):

- **Total aGDP** (all-time and trailing 7 days)
- **Total number of completed jobs** (all-time and trailing 7 days)
- **Total unique active wallets** (trailing 7 days)
- **Average jobs per day** across the ecosystem
- **Average revenue per job** (Total Revenue / Total Jobs = implied average price point)

These tell us: What is the typical price point in the ACP economy? If average revenue per job is $0.50, our $0.25 Quick Filter is right in the pocket. If it's $5.00, we might be underpricing.

## **PRIORITY 3**: Top 150 Agent Census (Critical for Outreach List)

From the ACP Agents tab, sorted by aGDP:

For the top 50 agents (at minimum), capture:
- Agent name
- Agent role (Provider / Hybrid / Buyer)
- Primary service category
- Total aGDP
- Weekly job volume
- Whether they are graduated (Agent-to-Agent) or sandbox
- Number of job offerings listed

**What we're looking for:**
- How many of the Top 150 are **Buyers** or **Hybrid** (i.e., agents that purchase services from others)? These are our customers.
- How many are in the **DeFi / Trading / Treasury** category? These have the highest need for whitepaper verification.
- How many are in the **Intel / Research** category? These are either competitors or complementary services.
- How many are **Providers only** (sellers that don't buy)? These are unlikely to be our customers.

## **PRIORITY 4**: Cluster Information (Strategic for Positioning)

For each active cluster:
- Cluster name and type
- Member agents
- How member agents are selected (is it open application? Invitation? Contestable?)
- Revenue flow within the cluster

The Autonomous Hedge Fund cluster is our primary cluster target. We want to know: is the "Insights/Alpha Aggregator" seat filled? If so, by whom, and at what price? If not, we should plan to apply.

## **PRIORITY 5**: Butler Usage Data (Human Channel Sizing)

- How many active Butler users (the 50k+ Virgens number — is this current?)
- What types of services do Butler users request most?
- Average spend per Butler session
- Does Butler surface provider agents by price, rating, or category?

This tells us how much revenue to expect from the human channel (Butler) vs. the agent channel (A2A).
